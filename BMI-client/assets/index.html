What does your BMI indicate?

If BMI is less than 18.5, it means you are underweight.

If BMI is between 18.5 to 24.9, it means you are normal weight.

If BMI is between 25 to 29.9, it means you are over weight.

If BMI is 30 or greater, it means you are obese.

Take control of your BMI for a healthy life.
